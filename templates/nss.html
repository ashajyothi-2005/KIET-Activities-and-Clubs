National Service Scheme (NSS)

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a public service program aimed at developing the personality and social responsibility of students through community service. Activities include blood donation camps, literacy drives, and environmental awareness programs.

BY JOINING NSS:
  • Contribute meaningfully to community development and welfare activities.
  • Improve leadership, teamwork, and communication skills.
  • Learn to manage time, work under pressure, and handle real-world challenges.
  • Build a strong sense of social responsibility, discipline, and empathy.
Usage of NSS in Future (Job-Oriented Process):
  • Personality and Skill Development: Leadership, teamwork, communication, time & resource management, problem-solving.
  • Government Jobs: Preference or bonus marks in police, paramilitary, teaching jobs, and armed forces.
  • NGOs & Social Work: Preferred in NGOs, CSR departments, human rights, rural development sectors.
  • Scholarships & Higher Education: Value-added activity for admissions, scholarships, fellowships.
  • Interviews & Campus Placements: Shows maturity, social awareness, and leadership potential.
